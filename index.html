About Us

A warm Welcome from 'The Hope Trust Foundation.' We, from Amravati, Maharashtra are a group of students willing to join the few devoted hands in the society working for us all by giving crucial contributionns to the society. Working hard while we sleep in peace inside our homes. Our mission is to do the best possible efforts in saving our Mother Earth and have the vision of maximising our contributions, being responsible as a youth of today. So, why to delay good things. We are prepared to do whatever it takes. Yes, it will take time to come true, but it certainly will. All we need is your crucial support in this war of all.

About HTF

Understanding the urgency to act for conservation of nature along with uplifting the weaker section of society , youth group came together on 1st Aug 21 forming HTF. Our object is to work with grassroot initiatives for effecting positive changes in every aspect of the society. We believe development occurs when we grow together.
